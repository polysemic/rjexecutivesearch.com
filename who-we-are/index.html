Who We Are

  • Our organization values relationships and knows how important it is to build trust between potential employees and employers. We are confident that our placements will go above and beyond your expectations. We take a personal approach to our customer service by promising our clients loyalty and complete transparency throughout the entire recruiting process. Transcending your satisfaction is our goal.

    Our recruiting services are focused on a wide variety of industries partnering with organizations of all sizes. Our proven track record of success combined with our unique process differentiates us from other recruiting firms and includes the following: 

    • We have over 45 years of combined experience in the recruiting industry
    • Our recruiting team has niche expertise that aligns with your organizations specific industry
    • Our internal talent management system gives us greater access to passive candidate referrals from those we have already interviewed or placed, which has resulted in presenting qualified candidates on all searches within 15-20 business days..
    • Results-oriented with focus on a return on your investment in our services. We recruit from a unique and often exclusive passive candidate pool of top performers who are not active in their job search including direct recruitment from your competitors. 
    • Every top candidate must complete a DISC assessment that measures behaviors, motivators, acumen, competencies & emotional intelligence. Through the use of these assessments, we can ensure the candidates we present will be a successful fit.
